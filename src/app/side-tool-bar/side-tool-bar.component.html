Quick Rules

  1. Raise your bet
  2. Two cards are dealt to your hand : You can split that hand if the two cards have the same value.
    Next to your cards is the Cards Value, get it as close to 21 as possible by doubling or hitting
  3. Mr. House draws cards until he reaches 17, your wins and losses are calculated
  4. Earn your chips, start a new round

House rules

  • Blackjack pays 3:2
  • Wins are dealt at the end of the round
  • No insurrance
  • You can split, up to three hands
  • Only one deck is used at a time , you may try to count cards
